Maggy Stab 2.0

  • Originality:
  • This game is based on my first game, Maggy Stab 1.0.
  • Technical Merit:
  • I think the puzzle type is at least somewhat uniqe. The game works a lot better now, and is closer to my original vision.
  • Prototype Postmortem:
  • I feel like I'm getting a lot more comfortable with Phaser and JavaScript.
  • Prototype Assets:
    • All graphics were made from scratch.
    • All sound effects are from FreeSound.org
    • I used the code and tutorial from this address to build my "game" https://www.emanueleferonato.com/2014/08/28/phaser-tutorial-understanding-phaser-states/
  • Prototype Closest Other Game:
  • Stabby Mag 1.0
  • High Concept:
  • You're a magnet in a world of knives. Don't get stabbed!
  • Theme: Sometimes people love you so much it hurts!
  • Mandated Variety:
  • Only variety is what I decided to put into this particular level.
  • Prototype Goal:
  • Make something better out of my first game.
  • Player Experience Goals:
  • Avoid the knives, make it to the smiley.
  • Gameplay:
  • Cursor keys only. Left to move left. Right to move right. Up to jump.
  • Strategies:
  • The knives only begin to move towards the player if the player is within a certain distance of the knive.
  • Additionally, the kinves speed of movement directly coorelates to how close to the knive the player is.
  • Story/Setting/Premise:
  • Maggy the Magnet is a rockstar in the metal world. However, all his fans are knives, and if they get too close Maggy will die a horrible, painful death.
  • Target Audience:
  • People who like evasion and escape puzzles
  • Play Time:
  • One level to beat now, but there is potential for more.
