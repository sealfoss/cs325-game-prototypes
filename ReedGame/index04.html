Flappy Guy 2.0

  • Originality:
  • This game is actually a game, based on last weeks "game", that wasn't so much of a game at all in retrospect.
  • Technical Merit:
  • The flappy guy flaps and plays a sound at the same time.
  • Prototype Postmortem:
  • I was really glad to be able to make something worth looking at for more than a few seconds, unlike last week's "game".
  • Prototype Assets:
    • All graphics were made from scratch.
    • I used a Jump sound effect from FreeSound.org
    • I used the code and tutorial from this address to build my "game" https://www.emanueleferonato.com/2014/08/28/phaser-tutorial-understanding-phaser-states/
  • Prototype Closest Other Game:
  • Flappy Guy 1.0
  • High Concept:
  • Be a flappy guy, don't get zapped!.
  • Theme: You're a flappy guy in a lightening storm!
  • Mandated Variety:
  • Initial position of all the lightening bolts is random, other than that they spawn at the top of the screen.
  • Prototype Goal:
  • Make something better out of last week's game.
  • Player Experience Goals:
  • Avoid the lightening bolts.
  • Gameplay:
  • One player. 'W' jumps. 'A' moves the player left. 'D' moves the player right.
  • Strategies:
  • Avoid the lightening bolts as long as you can.
  • Story/Setting/Premise:
  • Flappy guy is flapping through the sky, when out of nowhere there's a lightening storm! Watch out for the lightening! Don't get zapped!
  • Target Audience:
  • People who don't mind being stressed out.
  • Play Time:
  • As long as you can survive.
