Flappy Guy

  • Originality:
  • Flappy bird is a much more advanced version of this game. #facts
  • Technical Merit:
  • The flappy guy flaps and plays a sound at the same time.
  • Prototype Postmortem:
  • Figuring out states was a lot harder for me than I thought it would be. Lot of trial and error. In the end, though, I feel like I've gotten a good handle on it.
  • Prototype Assets:
    • All graphics were made from scratch.
    • I used a Jump sound effect from FreeSound.org
    • I used the code and tutorial from this address to build my "game" https://www.emanueleferonato.com/2014/08/28/phaser-tutorial-understanding-phaser-states/
  • Prototype Closest Other Game:
  • Flappy Bird?
  • High Concept:
  • Be a flappy guy, don't get zapped!.
  • Theme: You're a flappy guy in a lightening storm!
  • Mandated Variety:
  • Initial position of all the ants is random, as is the movement of all NPC ants.
  • Prototype Goal:
  • Effectively use game states to manage the flow of play..
  • Player Experience Goals:
  • Effectively navigate a main menu, game play, and game over states.
  • Gameplay:
  • One player. You must left click on flappy guy to get him to fly. The objective is to avoid the lightening bolt. Flap your wings to freedom!
  • Strategies:
  • Flap your wings at the appropriate times in order to avoid the lightening bolt.
  • Story/Setting/Premise:
  • Flappy guy is flapping through the sky, when out of nowhere there's a lightening storm! Watch out for the lightening! Don't get zapped!
  • Target Audience:
  • People who like chance, and as little interactivity as possible in their video games.
  • Play Time:
  • Seconds, max.
